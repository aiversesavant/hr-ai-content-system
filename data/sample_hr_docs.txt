Employee Benefits Policy
Effective Date: Jan 2024
Department: HR
All full-time employees are eligible for 20 days of paid time off (PTO) annually.
Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days into the next year.
Employees must request PTO at least 3 days in advance.

Parental Leave Policy
Effective Date: Jan 2024
Department: HR
Employees are eligible for 12 weeks of paid parental leave.
Leave must be approved by HR and the employee’s manager.

Sick Leave Policy
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually.
Unused sick leave does not carry over.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote roles require HR approval.

Confidentiality Policy
Employees must not share sensitive information including SSN, salary, or personal data.

Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior and follow company ethics policies.

Benefits Enrollment
Employees must enroll in benefits within 30 days of joining.

Manager Approval Policy
All leave requests must be approved by the reporting manager.

Termination Policy
Employees must provide 2 weeks notice before resignation.

Data Privacy Policy
All employee data must be handled securely and in compliance with company policies.
